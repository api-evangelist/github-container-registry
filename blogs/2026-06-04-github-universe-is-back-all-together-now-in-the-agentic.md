GitHub Universe, the company's flagship developer conference, is returning to Fort Mason Center in San Francisco on October 28-29, 2026, with a focus on the agentic era. The event brings together builders, maintainers, security practitioners, and technical leaders for keynotes, panels, workshops, and networking opportunities centered around AI agents and collaborative software development workflows.
